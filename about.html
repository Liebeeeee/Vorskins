About Us

VOrskins is a beauty e-commerce where customers can find beauty products and sellers can sell their own beauty products.

VOrskins was founded in 2020 where our country started a pandemic which is COVID-19. At the start of pandemic, people struggled about their face treatment considering that COVID still dangerous and people still afraid to find their beauty product. Therefore, VOrskins was founded to be a beauty product market.

Vision

VOrskins has a vision to satisfying customers to their beauty and to be the beauty product shopping center. VOrskins has another vision to become one of the unicorn company that will be known all over the world.

Mission

VOrskins will develop their operational performance so customers will have their satisfaction about VOrskins performance. VOrskins will spread their names all over the world and VOrskins will work together with other big beauty companies.
